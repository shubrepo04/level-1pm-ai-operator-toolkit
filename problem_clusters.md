Real Problem Clusters
Cluster	What it Combines	Included Problems
1. **Fragmented Tools & Information**	Everything related to scattered systems and lack of a single source of truth	Too many tools, scattered feedback, version confusion, incomplete bug context
2. **Lack of Visibility & Alignment**	Not knowing what’s happening, who’s doing what, or where things stand	Unclear ownership, poor progress visibility, misleading metrics, ignored reports
3.**Communication Breakdown**	Inefficient or overwhelming communication patterns	Message overload, long unproductive meetings, async confusion
4. **Broken or Manual Workflows	Processes** that are inefficient, repetitive, or not standardized	Manual CRM work, poor onboarding, unclear workflows
5. **Time & Coordination Friction**	Delays caused by remote work structure itself	Time zone gaps, outdated updates, lack of real-time sync
